Pros and Cons of Adopting a Pet as a College Student

Pros

  • Having a pet such as a dog is scientifically proven to reduce stress
  • The offer a social advantage when it comes to meeting people on campus
  • They're a great way to stay motivated and excersice
  • Dogs are a great companion from anyone not just college students

Cons

  • Having a pet is a hefty time commitment some college students can't pull off
  • They come with their fair share of expenses that might not be in budget
  • College dorms usually do not allow pets unless they are ESAs

Personal Story

I adopted a dog to use as an ESA after my first semester. I had fallen into a pretty heavy depression and was looking things to help me out of it. I had been seeing a counselor and was already on medication. After my own research and heavy consideration I decided to adopt a dog. I was lucky enough to qualify for an ESA, so I had the opportunity to keep her in the dorms. It was the best possible decision I could have made, and I hope more college students consider adopting a companion.
